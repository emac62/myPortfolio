I started programming while still in high school, in the late 1970s, using punched cards! After a 35 year break to have a teaching career, here I am back at a computer terminal proving that I truly am a life long learner.

In developing my second career, I am grateful to all the programmers who have shared their skills.

Programming Student

Since my retirement from teaching in January 2018, I turned to the internet to learn to program computers. My first goal was to develop an app for the App Store. Learning Swift from Ray Wenderlich and Hacking with Swift was fun, exciting and never ending! Wanting more, I turned to Harvard's CS50 course through Edx and Corey Schaffer for the basics of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, Flask and Django. I learned just enough to put up a couple of websites.
